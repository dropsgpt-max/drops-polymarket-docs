**Answer:** The features for monitoring **Liquidity Pools**, **Whois data**, and detailed **Wallet Balances** have been discontinued. This decision was made due to low user engagement with these specific functionalities, coupled with the exceptionally high resource consumption they required.

## How to Link Your DropsTab Account with Drops Bot?

**Answer:** Linking your DropsTab and Drops Bot accounts is essential for participating in Fomo App tasks or [receiving specific alerts](../core-features/settings.md#miniapp-alerts). Follow these steps to establish the connection:

1. **Access DropsTab Website:** Open the [DropsTab](https://dropstab.com/).
2. **Log In:** Authorize your account if you haven't already.
3. **Navigate to Profile Settings:**
   * Click on your **avatar** (profile picture).
   * From the dropdown menu, select **Profile Settings**.
4. **Initiate Connection:**
   * In the **Linked Accounts** section, click the **Connect** button.
   * A menu will appear, offering two options: **Open Bot to Connect** or **copy the Verification Code**.
5. **Complete Linking (Recommended Method):**
   * The most reliable method for linking accounts is to **copy the Verification Code** from the DropsTab menu.
   * Paste and send this code in a private chat with the Drops Bot.
6. **Confirmation:** Upon successful linkage, you will receive the following message from the bot:

> ✅ Your Telegram Account has been successfully linked with your DropsTab Account.

Your linked account will also be displayed in the Linked Accounts section within your DropsTab Profile Settings.

## How do I disable the green **Buy** button (Caller Mode) in alerts?

**Solution:**\
To completely disable the **Buy button (Caller Mode)** in Drops Bot, follow these steps:

1. Open the bot’s **Main Menu**
2. Tap "⚙️ **Settings"**
3. Tap the "**☎️ Caller Mode"** button
4. Set it to "**Off"**

Once disabled, the green **Buy** button will no longer appear in **any of your alerts or profiles**.\
You can re-enable it at any time by returning to the same setting.

> **Note:** Disabling Caller Mode affects **all groups and notifications** linked to your current bot instance.

This button is part of the **Caller Mode** feature, which embeds your **referral link** into Solana token alerts.\
For a detailed explanation of how Caller Mode works and how to earn with it, see the [**Caller Mode section**](../advanced-tools/caller-mode.md).

# 🚀 Getting started

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
**Launch the Bot**

Open [@Drops](https://t.me/drops) in Telegram. It will connect to your account automatically.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
**Open the Menu**

Use the **Main Menu** to access features like tracking, alerts, trading, and profiles.

### 🎟️ Referrals

Opens the **Referrals** menu, where you can:

* Copy your **referral link**
* Track rewards in real time
* Access **Caller Mode**
* Start earning from:
  * **30% of every subscription** made by your referrals
  * **Up to 30% of trading fees** from their regular activity
  * **40% of trading fees** if they trade via your **Caller Link**

Your referrals also receive a **10% discount** on both subscriptions and trading fees.

> 💡 Power user? Have 10,000+ followers on Twitter or Telegram? Contact us for **exclusive deals**.

To learn more, see the [**Referrals section**](../advanced-tools/referrals.md) and [**Caller Mode section**](../advanced-tools/caller-mode.md).

### &#x20;☎️ Caller Mode

**Caller Mode** is a special feature for earning **boosted referral commissions** via **Solana trading activity** in Telegram groups.

* Embed your **Caller Link** into **SOL token alerts**
* Earn **40% of trading fees** per trade via your link — even if the user already has another referrer
* Works in any group where Drops Bot is active
* Fully automated and tied to your referral account

Learn more in the [**Caller Mode guide**](../advanced-tools/caller-mode.md).

# 🧩 Slash Commands

Drops Bot supports a powerful set of **slash commands** for instant access to all major features, dashboards, and automation tools — no menus required.\
Whether you’re a power user or just want faster control, here’s everything you need:

***

### 🧩 **General Bot Commands**

* `/menu` — Open the **main bot menu**
* `/add` — View **all available categories** to add: coins, wallets, NFTs, pools, patterns
* `/edit` — View **all categories you can edit**
* `/help` — Display a full guide, including:
  * Bot command list
  * Support links & chats
  * Access to:
    * Announcement Channel
    * 🇬🇧 English Chat / 🇷🇺 Russian Chat
    * Medium
    * YouTube
    * ➕ Add Bot to Group button

***

### 📊 **Monitoring Dashboards**

*   `/coins` — View your tracked **coins** with current price & percent change (1h / 12h / 24h)

    > If no coins are added, the bot prompts: **“Add Token”**
*   `/wallets` — Shows your tracked **wallets**

    > If empty, you’ll see **“➕ Add new”**
*   `/nft` — Displays tracked **NFT collections** with floor price & changes

    > If none added, the bot suggests using **“💎 NFT”**

***

### 📈 **Data & Utility Tools**

* `/funding` — Shows current **Binance Futures funding rates** (USDT pairs), updated every 8h
* `/gas` — Displays **Ethereum gas prices** by tier:
  * 🚀 Fast (\~30s)
  * 🚗 Average (\~3m)
  * 🐢 Low (>10m)\
    Includes **“Refresh”** button
* `/customize` — Configure which **metrics appear in wallet alerts**
* `/profiles` — View and manage your **Profiles** for group/channel notification routing
* `/gmx_positions` — Displays open **GMX trading positions** from tracked wallets

***

### 🧠 **Smart Search & Token Info**

* `/pumpfun` — View your **Pump.Fun Alert settings**: Raydium listing, trading volume triggers
* `/api` — Access the **API interface**, including:
  * Your API key
  * Webhook configuration
  * Subscription plans & payment flow

***

### 🧪 **DEX & CEX Token Search**

* `/btc`, `/bitcoin`, `/eth`, etc. — Check real-time price, volume & market cap for **CEX-listed coins**\
  Includes buttons:
  * **Chart and Info** (DropsTab)
  * **Refresh**
* `/0x...` — Lookup **DEX-listed tokens** by contract address (ETH, BSC, Polygon, Fantom, Arbitrum, Avalanche)\
  &#xNAN;_&#x45;xample:_\
  `/0xd2568accd10a4c98e87c44e9920360031ad89fcb`\
  ➤ Returns current USD price & change over 1h / 24h / 7d

***

### 💸 **Trading Commands**

* `/trading` — Open the **main trading dashboard**
* `/mywallets` — View and manage your **trading wallets**
* `/buy` — Open the interface to **buy a token**
* `/sell` — Open the interface to **sell a token**
* `/positions` — Check your wallet’s current **open trading positions**

***

With these slash commands, you can unlock full control over Drops Bot features in **seconds** — without digging through menus.\
Ideal for active traders, admins, and power users who want a clean, fast, and command-driven experience.

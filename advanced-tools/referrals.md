# 🎟️ Referrals

## **What is Referrals?**

Welcome to the **Drops Bot Referrals Program** — your gateway to **consistent and scalable earnings**.\
By inviting new users, you can **monetize** both their **subscription purchases** and **trading activity**.

## 💸 Referral Rewards Structure

### **💰 Standard Referral Benefits**

Users who sign up with your **Referral Code** receive a **10% discount** on all purchases and trades.\
As the referrer, you earn:

* **30% commission** from every subscription payment your referrals make
* **Up to 30%** from their trading fees — a **recurring revenue stream**

{% hint style="info" %}
The **10% discount** only applies to users you **invite directly** via your **referral code or link** — not through Caller Mode.
{% endhint %}

### **☎️ Caller Mode Rewards (SOL Only)**

Unlock **even higher rewards** with **Caller Mode**, built for **Solana (SOL)** token trading.

**How to activate:**

1. Go to the `🟢 Buy` section of any SOL token
2. Generate your unique **Caller Link**
3. Share this link directly, or add the bot to a group where alerts will include your Caller Link

**You earn 40% of the trading fee** from every trade made through your Caller Link.\
If the user has no previous referrer, they will be **permanently assigned to you**.

***

## 💵 Withdrawals

Once your referral balance reaches **$100.00**, you can request a payout via our **support team**.\
Withdrawals are **manual** and processed securely by the Drops team.

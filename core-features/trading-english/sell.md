# 🔴 Sell

In this menu, you can sell tokens by sending the **contract address** or a **link from DexScreener** (**SOL only**).

🔹 **Main Information Block**

* **Active Wallet** – displays the currently selected wallet (**can be changed via** `/change_tw`).
* **Wallet Balance** – available **SOL** amount and its equivalent in **USD**.
* **Positions** – total value of all tokens in the wallet in **SOL** and **USD**.

📦 **List of Available Tokens for Sale**\
Below the divider line, all available tokens are displayed, including:

* **Token Name (clickable, links to DexScreener)**.
* **Total token amount**, its value in **SOL**, and its equivalent in **USD**.

🔘 **Control Buttons Block**

* ↩ **Back** – returns to the previous menu.
* 🔄 **Refresh** – updates the token list, wallet balance, and current positions.
* 🟤 **\[Token]** – button with the token name; clicking it opens the token selling menu.

***

#### 2. Token Selling Menu

🔹 **Main Information Block**

* **Token Name (clickable)** – clicking opens the token page on **DexScreener**.
* **Token Contract** – displays the token address (**clickable**).
* **Current Token Price in USD**.
* **Market Cap** (market capitalization).
* **Liquidity** (available funds in the pool).
* **24h Trading Volume**.

📊 **Position Information Block**

* **Token Name (clickable)** – opens the token’s page on **DexScreener**.
* **Token Amount in SOL** (and its equivalent in **USD**).
* **Quantity** – total number of tokens in the wallet.
* **PNL (Profit and Loss)** – shows profit or loss since purchase:
  * 🟩 **Green Square** – if in profit.
  * 🟥 **Red Square** – if at a loss.
  * Displays **percentage change**, **X multiplier** of profit/loss, and the **difference in SOL** (converted to **USD**).
* **Spent** – total amount spent on buying this token (**SOL** and **USD**).
* **Avg Entry** – average entry price (**price at which the token was purchased**).

🔄 **Swap Block (Token-to-SOL Exchange)**

* Displays the number of **tokens being sold** and how much **SOL** will be received (**converted to USD**).
* **Token Balance** – shows the number of tokens in the wallet and their current value in **USD**.

🔘 **Control Buttons Block**

* ↩ **Back** – returns to the previous menu.
* 🔄 **Refresh** – updates token and sale information.
* **50% / 100%** – presets for quick token sale selection (**configurable in trading settings**).
* ✏ **X %** – enter a **custom swap amount** (**SOL** or percentage; last selected value is saved).
* ⚙️ **Slippage** – **adjust slippage percentage** (**last choice is saved**).
* 👛 **Wallet** – option to **select a different wallet or multiple wallets**.
* 🔴 **Sell** – **confirm token sale**.

***

#### 3. Post-Sale Menu

🔹 **Main Block**

* **Sold Token Name** and **amount sold**.
* **"Sold" (clickable)** – link to the transaction on the **blockchain explorer**.
* **Amount received in SOL** (converted to **USD**).
* **Updated wallet balance** after the sale (**SOL and USD**).

📊 **Partial Position Sale Information Block**

* **Token Name (clickable)** – displays the remaining position after the sale.
* **Remaining Token Amount** – in **SOL and USD**.
* **Quantity** – number of remaining tokens in the wallet.
* **PNL (Profit & Loss)** – shows profit or loss for the remaining position:
  * 🟩 **Green Square** – if in profit.
  * 🟥 **Red Square** – if at a loss.
  * Displays **percentage change**, **X multiplier** of profit/loss, and **difference in SOL** (converted to **USD**).
* **Spent** – total purchase cost of the tokens (**SOL and USD**).
* **Avg Entry** – average entry price for the remaining position.

📊 **100% Position Sale Information Block**

* 👜 **\[Ticker]** – token name (**clickable, links to DexScreener**).
* 🏁 **Position Closed** – position status (**since 100% was sold**).
* 🟩 / 🟥 **Realized PNL** – final profit or loss:
  * 🟩 **If in profit** – displays **% change**, **X multiplier**, and **final profit in SOL (+USD)**.
  * 🟥 **If at a loss** – displays **% change**, **X multiplier**, and **final loss in SOL (-USD)**.

📈 **Current Token Data Block**

* **Token Price** – at the last update.
* **Market Cap** – current token market capitalization.
* **Liquidity** – amount of liquid funds in the pool.
* **24h Trading Volume** – number of tokens bought and sold in the last 24 hours.

🔘 **Interaction Buttons Block**

* ↩ **Back** – returns to the **main menu**.
* 📦 **Your Positions** – navigates to the section of **all active positions**.

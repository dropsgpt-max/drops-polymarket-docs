# 💼 Your Positions

#### 1. **"Your Positions" Section**

This section displays all active positions in the current wallet, allowing for quick asset management.

🔹 **Top Block**

* **"Name Wallet" · SOL** – wallet name.
* ✏ **/change\_tw** – option to switch wallets.
* **Wallet Address** – public address of the wallet used for trading.
* **Balance:** – available **SOL** amount and its equivalent in **USD**.
* **Positions:** – current position balance in **SOL** and its equivalent in **USD**.

📊 **"Your Positions" Section (Displays All Tokens in the Wallet)**

* A **list of all tokens** currently held in the wallet.
* For each token, the following details are displayed:
  * **Token Name (clickable)** – opens the token’s information card.
  * **Balance in SOL and USD** – the token’s total value.
  * **Quantity** – total number of tokens in the wallet.

🔘 **Token Interaction Buttons**

* Tokens held in the wallet are displayed as buttons, allowing quick selection of the desired asset.
* Clicking on a token button **opens its interaction menu**.

🔹 **Buying Tokens**

* **0.1 / 0.5 SOL** – preset for token purchases (**values can be customized in settings**).
* **Buy X ✏** – enter a **custom amount** for purchasing tokens.

🔹 **Selling Tokens**

* **Sell 50% / 100%** – preset for token sales (**values can be customized in settings**).
* **Sell X ✏** – enter a **custom amount** for selling tokens.

🔘 **Additional Buttons**

* ↩ **Back** – returns to the **main menu**.
* 🔄 **Refresh** – updates balance and current position information.

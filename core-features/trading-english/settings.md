# ⚙️ Settings

#### 1. **"Settings" Section**

The **Settings** section allows you to configure purchase, sale, fee, and balance display preferences.

🔹 **SOL Settings Block**

* 🟢 **Buy Settings** – purchase settings (**opens a submenu**).
* 🔴 **Sell Settings** – sale settings (**opens a submenu**).
* **Priority Fee** – gas priority fee settings.
  * **Available options:**
    * **Fast** – low fee, slow transaction execution.
    * **Turbo** – medium fee and speed.
    * **Ultra** – high fee, fastest transaction processing.
* **MEV Protection** – protection against **MEV bots**.
  * **ON** – enables protection, reducing the risk of arbitrage from MEV bots.
  * **OFF** – disables protection, transactions proceed normally.

📌 **General Settings**

* **Tx Delays** – transaction delay setting.
  * Works only for **swaps/transfers between two wallets**.
  * If using a **single wallet**, the transaction executes immediately.
* **Show Small Balances** – display of small balances.
  * **ON** – shows balances/positions below **$10** in the list.
  * **OFF** – hides small remaining balances.

***

#### 2. **"Buy Settings" Menu**

* This section allows you to configure **quick-buy buttons** in **SOL**.
* **Buy Slippage** – sets **purchase slippage** (**default: 5%**).

***

#### 3. **"Sell Settings" Menu**

Similar to **Buy Settings**, but for sales.

* **Quick buttons** for selecting the **sale percentage**.
* **Sell Slippage** – sets **sale slippage** (**default: 5%**).

***

🔘 **Interaction Buttons**

* 🔙 **Back** – returns to the **previous menu**.

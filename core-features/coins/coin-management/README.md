# ⚙️ Coin Management

Welcome to the **Coin Management** section, your central hub for customizing and controlling how you track your cryptocurrency assets with Drops Bot. This area empowers you to fine-tune your monitoring experience, ensuring you receive the precise alerts and data you need, exactly when you need them.

Whether you're looking to adjust notification settings for a specific token, set up custom price triggers, or manage alerts for DEX swaps and funding rates, this section provides all the necessary tools and guidance.

**In this section, you will learn how to:**

* **Access Coin Settings:** Understand the different ways to enter the configuration mode for your tracked coins, including quick commands and menu navigation.
* **Configure Coin Alerts and Parameters:** Explore a comprehensive list of all available settings for your coins, such as price change alerts, swap notifications, funding rate parameters, and more.

Dive into the subsections to gain full control over your crypto monitoring preferences.

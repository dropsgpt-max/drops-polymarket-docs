### ⛽️ Gas Price Levels Explained

After opening the menu, you’ll see the current gas price categorized into three speed levels:

#### 🚀 **Fast (\~30 seconds)**

* **Highest priority** for immediate confirmation.
* Best for **urgent transactions** where speed is critical.
* Estimated processing time: **\~30 seconds**.

#### 🚗 **Average (\~3 minutes)**

* **Balanced** gas price for cost and speed.
* Suitable for **standard usage**.
* Estimated confirmation time: **\~3 minutes**.

#### 🐢 **Low (>10 minutes)**

* **Lowest cost**, but slowest processing time.
* Ideal for **non-urgent operations**.
* Transactions may take **10+ minutes** to confirm.

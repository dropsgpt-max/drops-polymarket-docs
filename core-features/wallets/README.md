# 👛 Wallets

## Why Monitor Wallets with Drops Bot?

Whether managing your own portfolio or observing market dynamics, Drops Bot provides crucial intelligence:

* **Personal Tracking:** Monitor incoming payments, received airdrops, completed trades, and detect unauthorized transactions to safeguard your assets.
* **Market Intelligence:** Spot potential investments, track whale movements in NFTs or tokens, identify early opportunities from new contracts, observe ICO fund collection, and follow token unlocks or wallets linked to major events.

## 💡 Key Monitoring Capabilities

Drops Bot equips you with essential features for in-depth wallet analysis:

* **Real-time Alerts:** Receive instant notifications for diverse wallet activities including swaps, transfers, NFT actions, and contract interactions.
* **Advanced Filtering:** Customize what you track with precision filters for [transaction value](wallet-management/configuring-wallet-alerts-and-parameters.md#wallet-specific-settings-edit-mode), [types (IN/OUT)](wallet-management/configuring-wallet-alerts-and-parameters.md#wallet-specific-settings-edit-mode), [specific events](wallet-management/configuring-wallet-alerts-and-parameters.md#wallet-specific-settings-edit-mode), and [allowed/hidden addresses](wallet-management/configuring-wallet-alerts-and-parameters.md#wallet-specific-settings-edit-mode).
* **Multi-chain Support:** Track across a wide array of EVM chains, Solana, Tron, TON, and more.
* **Custom Alert Formatting:** Personalize the content and structure of your wallet notifications for better signal clarity.
* **GMX Integration:** Directly track open GMX positions from your monitored wallets.

## ⚙️ Wallet Management & Customization

The bot provides comprehensive tools to manage your wallet tracking preferences:

* **Individual Wallet Settings:** Fine-tune alerts per wallet, including [filters](wallet-management/configuring-wallet-alerts-and-parameters.md#wallet-specific-settings-edit-mode), networks, and custom labels.
* **Bulk Actions:** Efficiently apply settings to [multiple wallets at once](wallet-management/accessing-wallet-settings.md#bulk-editing-multiple-wallets) with features like Mass Edit and Edit All.
* **Data Export:** Easily [export](export-wallets.md) your full list of tracked wallets for external analysis.
* **Advanced Display Options:** Customize how alerts appear with various data fields, chart sources, swap formats, and emoji preferences.

## ⚡️ Quick Access

Utilize these commands for rapid navigation and interaction with your wallet monitoring system:

* `/wallets` – View your list of tracked wallets.
* `/add` – Quickly add a new wallet.
* `/edit` – Access wallet settings.
* `/gmx_positions` – View open GMX positions.
* `/gas` – View Ethereum gas prices.
* `/help` – Open the support menu.
